The TIP Framework
A thinking tool for GP essay analysis — Singapore A Levels
Core idea
Every GP essay question is anchored in either a Trend or an Impact. Identifying which one anchors your question determines where your arguments come from — because arguments are always built on the opposite layer. P Perspectives enrich both paths by showing who is affected unequally, and who holds the power to change things.

Identify the anchor
What is the question centred on?
Trend anchor
A force or development driving change — e.g. technology, globalisation, social media, urbanisation.
Impact anchor
A condition or outcome — e.g. inequality, loss of privacy, a place for traditional arts, harm vs good.

Note: values-based questions
Questions centred on a contested value — freedom of speech, justice, equality — are Trend-anchored in disguise. The value is the ambient condition. Ask: what Trends enact or erode this value? What Impacts result? Then evaluate those to form your argument.
